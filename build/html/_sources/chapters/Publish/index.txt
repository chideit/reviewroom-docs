.. _Publish:

Publish
=======

Beside the Preview button is the Publishing, where all survey options reside. The Publish page includes contains benefitial options to further increase your survey exposure, such as set a survey password, customize the finish and termination pages, limit to 1 response per computer, etc. The Publish page is split into 2 tabs, where each contains its own instance of options.

This chapter will prep you for ensuring that you survey has the appropriate security features enabled, and desired survey response style. As a result, this chapter will teach you how to:

* Change your Deployment URL
* Purchase Responses
* Adjust security settings 
* Enable Redirection
